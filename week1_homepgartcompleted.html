About Me


Heyo~

My name is Melanie Li and I am majoring in Media Studies with a concentration of Emerging Media and minoring in Computer Science. Initially, I was on my to major in Computer Science, but I realized I wanted to work more towards design, so I switched paths.

In my free time, I enjoy watching anime, reading man(ga/hwa/hua) and webtoons, playing Genshin Impact, and drawing. As a person who loves to visualize, I found myself engaging in two different mediums to produce my art through two-dimensions: traditional and digital. Currently, I am more focused on improving myself with digital illustration which are inspired by a variety of Japanese animations, graphic novels, and RPGs.


Art Gallery

Welcome to my Art Gallery!

I started my art hobby when I was in 4th grade with traditional art. Fast forward, I then picked up digital illustration when I was in 11th grade and ever since then, I haven't drawn traditionally as often. My art style has taken shape from being exposed to watching anime since my childhood. Currently, I am developing my art style so it can be distinct from the style seen in anime while maintaining my art in the two-dimensional space.

As you look through my pieces, I hope there are improvements from my past to my recent illustrations. Also, you'll notice that I am drawing more of Genshin Impact ever since the game was released. Enjoy!
